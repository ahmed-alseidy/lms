## 6. Pricing Strategy

- **Annual discount:** 15–20% off for yearly billing
- **Free trial:** 14-day trial, no credit card required
- **Freemium (optional):** 1 course, 10 students, 1 GB storage, platform branding required
- **Student-based alternative:** Base $19/mo + ~$0.25 per active student/month

---

## 7. Market Positioning (Egypt / MENA)

- Offer **EGP pricing** (converted monthly) if targeting Egypt
- **Local payment methods:** Already integrated (e.g. Fawaterak)
- **Value proposition:** Local payments, Arabic, competitive pricing vs Teachable/Thinkific in MENA
